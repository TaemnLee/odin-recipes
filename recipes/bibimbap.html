Bibimbap Recipe

This is a recipe for making a tasty Bibimbap!

Ingredients

  • Steamed rice
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Egg
  • Mushrooms
  • Seasoned Spinach
  • Seasoned Bean sprouts
  • Sesame Oil
  • Gochujang

Steps

  1. Slice the vegetables into long and thin sticks
  2. Pour them into the huge rice bowl
  3. Add some sesame oil and Gochujang based on your preference
  4. MIX THEM UP!!!
  5. Add fried egg on the top
