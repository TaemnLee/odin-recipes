Pasta Recipe

This is a recipe for making a tasty pasta!

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmasan cheese powder
  • Mushroom
  • Dried Oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put salt and olive oil into the boiled water
  2. Boil Spaghetti for about 8 minutes
  3. Wash the Spaghetti in cold water and remove the water from it
  4. Slice garlic, onion, and mushroom
  5. Mix the sliced vegetables, Spaghetti, and tomato sauce all together
  6. Add Parmasan cheese powder and dried oregano for the last step
  7. Easy & tasty pasta made!
