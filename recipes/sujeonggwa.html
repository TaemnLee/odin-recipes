Sujeonggwa Recipe

This is one of my favorite traditional Korean desert. It is well known for its bittersweet taste!

Ingredients

  • Cinammon
  • Pine nut
  • Ginger
  • Persimmon
  • Brown sugar
  • Water

Steps>

  1. Peel of the gingers
  2. Wash the gingers and cinammon
  3. Put the gingers and cinammon into a pot
  4. Fill up the water into the pot and boil it for about an hour
  5. Take out the ginger and cinammon when the color of the water is dyed enough
  6. Put the brown sugar into the water and boil about 20 minutes
  7. Cool the water and place persimmon and pine nut into the water
